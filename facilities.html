Department Facilities

Computer Laboratories

Our computer labs provide a space for learning, research, and innovation. Equipped with modern computers, essential software, and internet access, they support students and professionals in coding, design, research, and digital creativity. Whether for study, collaboration, or development, the labs ensure a productive and tech-driven environment.

Animation Laboratory

Our animation labs are designed for creativity and innovation, providing the tools needed for 2D and 3D animation. Equipped with high-performance computers, industry-standard software, and digital drawing tablets, these labs support students and professionals in bringing their ideas to life. Whether for animation, visual effects, or motion graphics, the labs offer a space for exploration and artistic expression.

Theatre

Our theatre provide a space for creativity, experimentation, and performance. Equipped with professional lighting, sound systems, and rehearsal areas, they support students and artists in developing their craft. Whether for acting, directing, or stage design, this place foster innovation and artistic expression in a dynamic environment.

Library

Our library is a gateway to knowledge, offering a diverse collection of books, digital resources, and study spaces. Whether you're researching, learning, or simply exploring new ideas, it provides a quiet and inspiring environment for discovery.
